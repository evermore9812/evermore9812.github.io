How to place an order?

Go to our Order page and select the items you wish to order, set the quantity, and fill our the form to complete your order. Your package will be delivered to you within 3 business days, by owl post.

How to pay for the order?

In case of an online order, you don't pay in advance. Once the owl has delivered the package, you put the exact amount of money in the owl's sack attached to its leg and the invoice appears in the package. If you forget to pay, be warned, that the owl may remind you politely - involving a bit of beak, feather and claws - and won't leave until the payment is completed. British Wizarding Currency is favoured, but you can pay in muggle money as well. The accepted muggle currencies are in the Exchange Rate chart bellow. You can ckeck the required payment when placing the order. At the end of the form, you can find the amount in several currencies freshly calculated.
