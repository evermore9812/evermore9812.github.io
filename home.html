Welcome to Weasley & Weasley aka Weasleys' Wizarding Wheezes, the most famous joke shop in Diagon Alley!

In 1994 a certain Mr. Potter won the Triwizard Tournament and gave his winning of 1000 Galleons to the Weasley twins. Fred and George started their business while still attending Hogwarts. Talent met the possibility and the start-up was up and running in no time. 1995, the twins senior year presented itself with a big, pink problem: Ms Dolores Umbridge, DADA teacher and Headmistress. After a while the Weasley twins felt that they outgrowed their alma mater and the time to step out into real life was upon them. They made an unforgettable exit and set off to fulfill their dreams. For years they were the most successful young businessmen of wizarding Britain.

They continued to provide joy and happinness as darker times arised. In the summer of 1997, the Death Eaters seized control of the British Ministry of Magic. In the spring of 1998, it was discovered that the Weasleys had been helping the fugitive Harry Potter, and the family went into hiding. Although forced to close the shop in Diagon Alley, Fred and George continued to sell their products via mail order. On the 2nd of May 1998, Fred and George Weasley fought for the light. As we know, the Dark Lord perished, but the brighter the day, the darker the shadows. Fred Weasley died of an explosion when fighting alongside his brother Percy Weasley.

George continued to operate the business after Fred's death, despite never fully getting over the loss of his twin. Two years after his youngest brother, Ronald (Ron) Weasley became an Auror, he left his job and joined George and they together turned the already successful emporium into an even more gigantic money-spinner.

The last 12 years were a huge progress both in the wizarding world, and in the life of the joke shop in Diagon Alley. After the success with the modified Wi-Fi routers in several wizarding community and in Hogwarts – you know, these are not likely to explode anymore when casting a spell in a hundred foot range of it -, we saw the opportunity to open a webshop. Not all products can be found here, first we are selling only the favourites online and in case of the favoured outcome we will expand our product range. To learn about ordering online visit our How to... page.
